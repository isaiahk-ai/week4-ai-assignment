## Question 1
What is Data? Explain in your own words and give two Examples.

Data is a collection of raw, unprocessed facts, figures, symbols, or observations.
Examples are: numbers, text, images, etc

## Question 2
Explain the difference between:
- Structured Data
- Unstructured Data

Give two Examples of each.

Structured data is highly organized and follows a predefined format or schema (like a database table), while unstructured data lacks a fixed format or model. Example of structured data are: Name, Date, and Price.
Example of unstructured data: Images, Audio file, and Video.
